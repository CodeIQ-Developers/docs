## Origen Masterjob Injection

<Callout type="info">
    Please note that it is essential to list the jobs from `origen_masterjob` on the commerces tab.
</Callout>

You must insert the next line at the bottom of the fxmanifest.lua on origen_masterjob resource.

```lua filename="fxmanifest.lua"
server_script  "@cq-lib/injections/server/origen_masterjob.lua"
```
